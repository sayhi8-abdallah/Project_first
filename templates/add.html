Adding A Skilll

For Adding A Skilll In Our Web You Need To Add The Skill in The Database, For This:

1- Entry in the source of page

2- Click in '/static/db/add.db'

3- After the download, install database browser

4- Go in the app and click in 'Browse Data'

5- Add your skill, progress and user_id in the table of skills

6- Add your name and user_id in the table of users

---------| Before This Steps, Your Skill Will Add In The Web |---------
